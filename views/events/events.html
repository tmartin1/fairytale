Transportation Schedule

Transportation is provided to and from any of the three featured hotels and all wedding events. Chartered motorcoaches will be available for transportation to the ceremony at the following times:

  • 6:30pm - Pickup at the Boardwalk Main Lobby
  • 6:45pm - Pickup at the Pop Century Main Lobby
  • 7:00pm - Pickup at the Port Orleans Main Lobby

Transportation from the reception back to your respective hotels will be provided from 11:30pm all the way until 2:00am. If you miss the last bus or are staying at a different hotel, you can Uber on Disney property- for much less than a taxi (and with an open bar, I hope you need a ride back to your hotel).

Formal attire requested: Suites/tuxes for men, floor length dresses/gowns for women.
* If you're staying at one of the featured Disney hotels, transportations to and from all wedding events will be taken care of.
