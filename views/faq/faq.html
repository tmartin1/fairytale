FAQs and Other Helpful Info

Will there be any dancing at the reception?

Absolutely! There will be plenty of opportunities to show off your dance moves. Seriously, we've recently started taking up ballroom dancing, and while we're not any good, we still love to dance- and would love for you to join us out on the dance floor. So dust off those shoes and get ready to waltz, foxtrot, rumba, and swing!


Multi-day park tickets DON'T have to be used on consecutive days

For example, if you get a 4 day park hopper ticket, you can go to the park Thursday, Friday, relax on Saturday, and then go back Sunday and Monday.

The only restriction is that you have to use the tickets within 14 days of the first day. Meaning you couldn't take that 4-day ticket and go to Magic Kingdom on say, Thursday, June 23rd; then you would have to use the remaining 3 days before Thursday, July 7th.


Use Disney's Magical Express if you're flying into Orlando

Make your life easier. If you're staying at any official Disney hotel (ask when you're booking if you're not sure) then Disney provides complimentary transportation to and from the airport with complimentary luggage delivery.

If you book your hotel and flight at different times- don't worry! You can still take advantage of this! Click here for more details about Disney's Magical Express.


FastPasses

First, FastPasses are FREE. They are included with your park ticket. You can book up to three fastpasses per day to start and you can get more after you use the first three you booked.

How do you book your fastpasses? You go to your MyDisneyExperience Account and click the FastPass+ link in the right dropdown menu. This is also where you can make dinning reservations, check/update hotel reservations, etc.

SUPER IMPORTANT: If you're staying at a Disney World hotel, you can book fastpasses as soon as 60 days before your first night. This is important, because if you don't want to wait 2 hours to ride the awesome roller coasters, you want to set a reminder to book your fastpasses at that 60 day mark.

Your Magic Band is your key to everything Disney

After you book your hotel reservation, Disney will send you a Magic Band! Your Magic Band is your hotel key, your park ticket, your fastpass, your photopass, the thing they scan for any dinning reservations, the thing you can use to pay for stuff (you have the option to link a card to it to use it to pay for anything in the parks), and probably even more cool stuff that I'm forgetting at the moment.
