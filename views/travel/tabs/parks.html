Magic Kingdom

Magic Kingdom is our favorite park. It truly embodies the essence of Disney magic. Filled with endless attractions, fantastic stage shows, parades, and incredible fireworks displays, Magic Kingdom is without question the happiest place on earth.

EPCOT

While it doesn’t feel like a typical theme park, EPCOT is comprised of two separate sections: Future World and World Showcase. Future World is where you’ll find most the rides, while the World Showcase is home to 11 country-themed pavilions with dining and shopping experiences.

Hollywood Studios

Designed to focus on the magic of Hollywood, Disney's Hollywood Studios gives visitors the opportunity to travel back in time to Hollywood's heyday of the 1930s and '40s. Hollywood Studios has the best roller coaster, Tower of Terror, and a Fantasmic! Fireworks show.

Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom is home to more than 250 different animals showcased throughout the park. It features an exciting safari ride where you can tour the wild of Africa and Asia as well as a thrilling roller coaster to the top of Mt. Everest! This and Magic Kingdom are the two most kid-friendly parks.
