There are a lot of fantastic places to stay during your time at Disney World. All official Disney resorts offer amazing benefits including complimentary transportation and luggage handling from Orlando International Airport via Disney’s Magical Express. You just walk off the plane and head straight to the bus- they’ll even deliver your bags to your hotel room for you!

While you are welcome to stay anywhere you like, wedding guests can get a special discount at the hotels listed below. Additionally, transportation to and from these hotels and all wedding events is already taken care of for you as well!

Disney’s Pop Century Resort $145.13 / night
Disney’s Port Orleans - Riverside $198.00 / night
Disney’s BoardWalk Resort $353.25 / night

Prices listed above include tax and are based on double occupancy.

To book your Disney World Resort accommodations, please call the Disney Reservation Team at (407) 827-6799. They can help you with park tickets and dining reservations too!

* If you choose to stay at an alternative Walt Disney World resort hotel, please let us know so we can notify our wedding coordinator.

** The Swan and Dolphin, Four Season, Shades of Green, and Disney Springs Resorts are NOT official Disney hotels and therefore do not use Disney’s Magical Express.
