Where to Stay

There are a lot of fantastic places to stay during your time at Disney World. All official Disney resorts offer amazing benefits including complimentary transportation and luggage handling from Orlando International Airport via Disney’s Magical Express. You just walk off the plane and head straight to the bus- they’ll even deliver your bags to your hotel room for you!

While you are welcome to stay anywhere you like, wedding guests can get a special discount at the hotels listed below. Additionally, transportation to and from these hotels and all wedding events is already taken care of for you as well!
