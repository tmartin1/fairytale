Fairytale dreams come true for children of all ages at Magic Kingdom park. Delight in classic attractions, enchanting fireworks, musical parades and beloved Disney Characters across 6 whimsical lands. See fantasy become a reality as you explore Adventureland, Frontierland, Liberty Square, Fantasyland, Tomorrowland, and Main Street USA. Zoom through space, become a swashbuckling pirate and watch fireworks light up the sky over Cinderella Castle.
<br>
<br>
Magic Kingdom is our favorite park. It truly embodies the essence of Disney magic. Filled with endless attractions, fantastic stage shows, parades, and incredible fireworks displays, Magic Kingdom is without question the happiest place on earth.
